Rhetorical Collage

This project is an ad for the band Linkin Park's latest single release, "Lost." I wanted
to focus on how we as humans go through hardships in life and how life can be challenging in
general. A lot of their music talks about dealing with life, the pain we experience, and bottling
up our emotions. I think that if some people give their music a listen, it could really benefit them
due to the messages expressed in their music, especially if that said person is going through a tough
time in their life.
